## Chair weights

Our outdoor chairs often blow over in the wind, so I have devised a way to weight them down without making them ugly.

https://img.incoherency.co.uk/6568

There is a white PVC tube filled with lead shot, slotted into some TPU 3d-printed end caps, screwed to the aluminium chair legs
with self-tapping screws.
